About Me

My name is Martha and I’m an illustrator based in Oakville, Ontario. I’ve been creating for as long as I can remember. From drawing on the walls to painting on a canvas, my entire life has been full of art and colour!

I’m passionate about travelling. Exploring different parts of the world has had a huge influence on my work. The love I have for my friends and family has also inspired my work. I often use mountains to represent the feeling of being grounded and having a support system. I love using yellow, pink and teal to create dream like illustrations and bring my audience to another world!

I’m always looking to team up with other creatives. My email is funnylittlehoney@gmail.com.
